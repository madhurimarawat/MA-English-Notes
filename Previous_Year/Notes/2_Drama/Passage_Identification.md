# 📘 DRAMA 1: DOCTOR FAUSTUS

### 🚨 IF YOU SEE:

• God / Heaven / Hell
• Lucifer / Belzebub
• Franciscan friar
• Soul being damned
• Despair in God
• Devil changing shape
• “Damned” / “Salvation”
• Clock striking midnight
• Fear of eternal punishment

➡️ ✅ IT IS **DR. FAUSTUS**

---

### 🔎 Apply to Your Examples:

(b) “Go, and return an old Franciscan friar...”
👉 Devil changing shape
👉 Religious disguise
➡️ ✅ DR. FAUSTUS

(c) “Despair in God and trust in Belzebub!”
👉 Belzebub mentioned
👉 Spiritual despair
➡️ ✅ DR. FAUSTUS

(d) “The spirit that I have seen may be a devil…”
👉 Fear of devil deception
➡️ ✅ DR. FAUSTUS

---

### 💡 QUICK MEMORY TRICK:

If it talks about:
SOUL + DEVIL + DAMNATION
= FAUSTUS

# 📘 DRAMA 2: HAMLET

### 🚨 IF YOU SEE:

• “To be or not to be”
• Fortune / suffering / conscience
• Ghost
• Skull / graveyard
• “O Gertrude!”
• Madness
• Arras
• Rapier
• Poison
• Queen / Denmark
• Emotional royal conflict

➡️ ✅ IT IS **HAMLET**

---

### 🔎 Apply to Your Examples:

(c) “To be or not to be…”
➡️ ✅ HAMLET (Soliloquy)

(d) “O what a noble mind is here overthrown!”
👉 Ophelia speaking about Hamlet
➡️ ✅ HAMLET

(e) “O Hamlet, speak no more!”
👉 Queen Gertrude
➡️ ✅ HAMLET

(f) “When sorrows come, they come not single spies…”
👉 Claudius speaking
➡️ ✅ HAMLET

(g) “Behind the arras… rat, a rat!”
👉 Polonius killed
➡️ ✅ HAMLET

---

### 💡 QUICK MEMORY TRICK:

If it mentions:
MADNESS + DENMARK + POISON + GERTRUDE
= HAMLET
