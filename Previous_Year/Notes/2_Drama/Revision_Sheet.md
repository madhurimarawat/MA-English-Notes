# **Drama Revision Sheet**

## **Doctor Faustus** – Christopher Marlowe

* **Age/Era:** Elizabethan Age (16th Century)
* **Type:** Tragic Drama, Morality Play
* **Main Idea:** A scholar sells his soul to the Devil for knowledge and power and faces tragic consequences.
* **Main Characters:** Faustus, Mephistophilis, Lucifer, Good Angel, Evil Angel.

---

## **The Tempest** – William Shakespeare

* **Age/Era:** Elizabethan Age
* **Type:** Romantic Comedy, Tragi-Comedy
* **Main Idea:** Prospero uses magic to restore justice and forgive his enemies.
* **Main Characters:** Prospero, Miranda, Ariel, Caliban, Ferdinand.

---

## **She Stoops to Conquer** – Oliver Goldsmith

* **Age/Era:** Neoclassical Age (18th Century)
* **Type:** Comedy of Manners, Sentimental Comedy (anti-sentimental)
* **Main Idea:** Mistaken identity leads to humorous situations and ends in marriage.
* **Main Characters:** Kate Hardcastle, Marlow, Tony Lumpkin, Mr. Hardcastle.

---

## **A Doll’s House** – Henrik Ibsen

* **Age/Era:** Modern Age (19th Century Realism)
* **Type:** Problem Play, Social Realistic Drama
* **Main Idea:** A woman realizes her oppression in marriage and leaves to find her identity.
* **Main Characters:** Nora Helmer, Torvald Helmer, Krogstad, Mrs. Linde.

---

## **The Zoo Story** – Edward Albee

* **Age/Era:** Modern Age (20th Century)
* **Type:** Absurd Drama, One-Act Play
* **Main Idea:** A lonely man tries to communicate in a meaningless modern world, ending in tragedy.
* **Main Characters:** Jerry, Peter.
