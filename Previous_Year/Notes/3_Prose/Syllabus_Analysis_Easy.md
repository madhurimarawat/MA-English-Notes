# 📚 English Prose Syllabus : Smart Summary + Analysis Guide

---

## 🔑 First: **D vs ND Questions**

### 📌 **D = Detailed**

👉 Study:

* Themes & moral lessons
* Style & rhetorical devices
* Examples & illustrations
* Historical or philosophical context
  ✅ **Long-answer (10–15 marks)** questions can come directly.

### 📌 **ND = Non-Detailed**

👉 Study:

* Central idea & main theme
* Key incidents/examples
* Short moral or philosophical takeaways
  ❌ No need for deep stylistic or literary analysis.

✨ **IMPORTANT TRUTH**
Most essays in this syllabus are **ND**, so you can score high even with minimal study.
