# 📘 PROSE 1: FRANCIS BACON – Selected Essays

(From *Essays* – Francis Bacon)

### 🚨 IF YOU SEE:

• Short, powerful maxims
• Advice about studies / reading / writing
• Truth vs lie discussion
• Friendship relieving sorrow
• Power and responsibility
• Practical worldly wisdom
• Aphoristic style
• Moral instruction without story

➡️ ✅ IT IS **BACON**

---

### 🔎 Quick Clue Words:

“Studies” / “Truth” / “Friendship” / “Great Place”
OR
Strong moral advice in 2–3 line statements

---

### 💡 MEMORY TRICK:

If it sounds like:
SMART + SHORT + MORAL ADVICE
= BACON
