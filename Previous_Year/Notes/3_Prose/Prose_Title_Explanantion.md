# Prose Title Explanation

## 1. Of Studies – Francis Bacon

📘 **Renaissance Age**

### 📌 Meaning of the Title:

* **Studies** = Reading, learning, and acquiring knowledge 📖
* Refers to the process of intellectual development 🧠

➡️ Means: *The importance and purpose of gaining knowledge*

---

### 🎯 Symbolism:

* Studies symbolize **intellectual growth and wisdom** 🌱
* They represent **self-improvement and mental training**
* Also symbolize **practical success in life**

---

### 🧠 Why the title fits perfectly:

The essay explains why and how people should study.

Example:

> “Reading maketh a full man…”

Shows studies develop knowledge and personality.

👉 Title reflects the **value, use, and purpose of education**

---

## 2. Of Truth – Francis Bacon

✨ **Renaissance Age**

### 📌 Meaning of the Title:

* **Truth** = Reality, honesty, and moral correctness ⚖️

➡️ Means: *The nature and importance of truth*

---

### 🎯 Symbolism:

* Truth symbolizes **divine light and wisdom** ☀️
* Lies symbolize **darkness and illusion**

---

### 🧠 Why the title fits perfectly:

The essay discusses why truth is important and why people avoid it.

Example:

> “What is truth? said jesting Pilate…”

Shows people question truth but avoid it.

👉 Title reflects the **philosophical discussion of truth**

---

## 5. The Spectator Essays – Joseph Addison & Richard Steele

📰 **Neoclassical Age**

### 📌 Meaning of the Title:

* **Spectator** = Observer 👁️
* Person who observes society and behavior

➡️ Means: *Observations on society and human behavior*

---

### 🎯 Symbolism:

* Spectator symbolizes **neutral observer of society**
* Represents **social criticism and moral guidance**

---

### 🧠 Why the title fits perfectly:

The essays observe and describe social life and manners.

Example:

> Sir Roger represents rural society.

👉 Title reflects the **observation of society and human nature**

## 6. Dream Children: A Reverie – Charles Lamb

🌙 **Romantic Age**

### 📌 Meaning of the Title:

* **Dream Children** = Imaginary children 👧👦
* **Reverie** = Daydream or deep thought 💭

➡️ Means: *Imaginary children seen in a dream*

---

### 🎯 Symbolism:

* Dream children symbolize **Lamb’s lost happiness and unreal life**
* Represent **unfulfilled desires**

---

### 🧠 Why the title fits perfectly:

The children disappear at the end, showing they are imaginary.

Example:

> Lamb realizes he never had children.

👉 Title reflects **memory, imagination, and loneliness**

---
