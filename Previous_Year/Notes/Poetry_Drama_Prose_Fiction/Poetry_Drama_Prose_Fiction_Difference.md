# 📚 Difference Between Poetry, Drama, Prose, and Fiction

These are the **four main forms of literature**. Each has its own **purpose, structure, and style**.

---

# 🟣 1. Poetry 📝🎶

## 🌸 Definition

Poetry is literature written in **verse form**, using rhythm, rhyme, and emotions.

It focuses on:

* Feelings ❤️
* Imagination 🌙
* Beauty 🌸

## 🧩 Structure

* Written in **lines and stanzas**
* May use rhyme and meter
* Uses figurative language

## ✨ Features

* 🎵 Rhythm and musical quality
* 🌄 Imagery (mental pictures)
* 🔥 Emotion-focused
* 🧠 Symbolism and metaphor

## 👩‍🎓 Example

📘 Poem: *Daffodils*
by William Wordsworth

Example lines:

> "I wandered lonely as a cloud"

## 🎯 Types of Poetry

* Lyric poetry 🎶
* Narrative poetry 📖
* Dramatic poetry 🎭
* Epic poetry ⚔️

---

# 🟣 2. Drama 🎭

## 🌸 Definition

Drama is literature written to be **performed on stage**.

It shows story through:

* Dialogue 💬
* Action 🏃
* Performance 🎭

## 🧩 Structure

* Written in **acts and scenes**
* Includes dialogues and stage directions

## ✨ Features

* 👥 Characters
* 💬 Dialogue
* 🎬 Stage performance
* ⚡ Conflict

## 👩‍🎓 Example

📘 Play: *Hamlet*
by William Shakespeare

Example line:

> "To be or not to be"

## 🎯 Types of Drama

* Tragedy 💀
* Comedy 😂
* Tragicomedy 😢😂

---

# 🟣 3. Prose 📖

## 🌸 Definition

Prose is literature written in **normal sentence form**, without rhyme.

It is the **most common form of writing**.

## 🧩 Structure

* Written in paragraphs
* Uses sentences normally

## ✨ Features

* 🧠 Logical structure
* 📚 Easy to understand
* 💬 Natural language

## 👩‍🎓 Examples

* Essays
* Novels
* Short stories
* Biographies

Example writer: Charles Lamb

Example work: *Dream Children*

---

# 🟣 4. Fiction 📘🌍

## 🌸 Definition

Fiction is prose that tells **imaginary stories**.

Not real, but feels real.

## 🧩 Structure

Has 5 main parts:

1. 🟢 Plot – story
2. 👤 Characters – people
3. 🌍 Setting – place
4. ⚡ Conflict – problem
5. 🎯 Theme – message

## 👩‍🎓 Example

📘 Novel: *Great Expectations*
by Charles Dickens

Character: Pip

---

# 🔥 MAIN DIFFERENCE (Detailed Explanation)

## Poetry vs Prose

| Poetry 📝         | Prose 📖              |
| ---------------- | -------------------- |
| Written in lines | Written in sentences |
| Uses rhyme       | No rhyme             |
| Emotional        | Logical              |
| Example: poem    | Example: essay       |

---

## Drama vs Fiction

| Drama 🎭                 | Fiction 📘           |
| ----------------------- | ------------------- |
| Written for performance | Written for reading |
| Uses dialogue           | Uses narration      |
| Acts & scenes           | Chapters            |

# 📖 Prose vs Fiction (Simple Relationship)

## 📚 Prose = Big category (umbrella) ☂️

Prose means **any writing in normal sentences and paragraphs** (not poetry).

It has **two main types**:

### 1. Fiction 📘 (imaginary)

Stories that are **not real**, created from imagination.

Examples:

* Great Expectations – imaginary story of Pip
* Robinson Crusoe – fictional survival story

Types of fiction:

* Novel 📗
* Short story 📕
* Novella 📙

---

### 2. Non-fiction 📄 (real)

Based on **real facts and real life**.

Examples:

* Essays ✍️
* Biography 👤
* Autobiography 📖

Example writer: Charles Lamb (essays are prose but not fiction)
