# 🌿 Georgian Poets

---

## 📖 1. Meaning of Georgian Poetry

**Definition:**
**Georgian poetry is the poetry written during the reign of King George V (1910–1936), focusing on nature, rural life, simplicity, and human emotions.**

**Easy Meaning:**
Georgian poetry = **simple poetry about nature 🌿, village life 🏡, and human feelings ❤️**

---

## 📅 2. Period of Georgian Poetry

📆 Time: **1910 – 1936**

Named after King George V

It came:

* After Victorian Age
* Before Modern Age

**Keywords:**
`Nature • Simplicity • Rural life • Pre-modern poetry`

## ✍️ 4. Major Georgian Poets (VERY IMPORTANT)

---

### 1. Rupert Brooke ⭐ (MOST IMPORTANT)

**Famous poem:**
👉 The Soldier

**Famous quote:**

> “If I should die, think only this of me”

**Meaning:**
Love and sacrifice for country

**Themes:**

* Patriotism 🇬🇧
* Love for country
* Nature

**Keywords:**
Patriotism • Nature • Emotion

---

### 2. Walter de la Mare

**Themes:**

* Nature
* Imagination

**Keywords:**
Nature • Dream • Imagination

---

### 3. John Masefield

**Famous poem:**
Sea Fever

**Famous quote:**

> “I must go down to the seas again”

**Meaning:**
Love for sea and nature

**Keywords:**
Nature • Sea • Adventure

---

### 4. W. H. Davies

**Famous poem:**
Leisure

**Famous quote:**

> “What is this life if, full of care”

**Meaning:**
People don’t enjoy nature because they are busy

**Keywords:**
Nature • Simple life • Anti-modern life
