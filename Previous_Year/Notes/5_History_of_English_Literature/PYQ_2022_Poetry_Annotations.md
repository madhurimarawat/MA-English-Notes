## 1️⃣

> Ful semly hir wympul pynched was,
> Hire nose tretys, hir eyen greye as glas,
> Hir mouth ful smal, and therto softr and reed ;
> But sikerly she hadde a fair forheed ;
> It was almoost a Spanne brood, I trowe ;
> For, hardily, she was nar undergrowe.

✅ **Poem:** The Canterbury Tales
✅ **Character:** The Prioress
✅ **Clue:** Middle English language + description of appearance

## 2️⃣

> For hym was levere have at his beddles heed
> Twenty bookes, clad in blak or reed, Of Aristotle and his philosophie,
> Than robes riche, or fithele, or gay sautrie.
> But al be that he was a philosophre,
> Yet hadde he but litel gold in cofre :

✅ **Poem:** The Canterbury Tales
✅ **Character:** The Oxford Clerk
✅ **Clue:** Loves books more than wealth

## 4️⃣

✅ **Event:** Satan and fallen angels in Hell
✅ **Clue:** Epic tone + Hell imagery
