# 🌄 The Age of Romanticism (1798–1832)

The Romantic Age began with the publication of Lyrical Ballads by William Wordsworth and Samuel Taylor Coleridge in 1798.
It was a reaction against the **Age of Reason**, focusing on **emotion, imagination, nature, and individual freedom**.

---

# 🧠 FLOWCHART: Rise of Romanticism

```
Age of Reason (1700–1798)
Focus on logic, intellect, satire
            ↓
Reaction against reason ❌🧠
Writers rejected cold logic
            ↓
Romantic Movement begins (1798)
Publication of Lyrical Ballads
            ↓
Focus on emotion, imagination, nature 🌿
Individual freedom and creativity
            ↓
Development of Romantic poetry and novels 📖
            ↓
Result:
Literature became emotional, imaginative, and nature-centered
```

# 1️⃣ Characteristics of Romanticism

### 🔹 Main Keywords & Features

* **Emotion over reason** ❤️
  Feelings became more important than logic

* **Love of nature** 🌿
  Nature seen as teacher, healer, and guide

* **Imagination and creativity** 💭
  Focus on imagination, dreams, and beauty

* **Individualism** 👤
  Focus on personal freedom and self-expression

* **Interest in common people** 🚶
  Focus on rural life and ordinary people

* **Supernatural elements** 👻
  Mystery, fantasy, and imagination

# ✍️ Exam-Ready 5-Line Summary

* Romantic Age began in 1798 with Lyrical Ballads.
