# **Fiction Revision Sheet**

## **Gulliver’s Travels** – Jonathan Swift

* **Age/Era:** Neoclassical Age (18th Century)
* **Type:** Satirical Novel, Political Allegory
* **Main Idea:** Travels of Gulliver to strange lands used to satirize human nature, politics, and society.
* **Main Characters:** Lemuel Gulliver, Lilliputians, Brobdingnagians, Houyhnhnms.

---

## **Pamela** – Samuel Richardson

* **Age/Era:** Neoclassical Age (18th Century)
* **Type:** Epistolary Novel, Sentimental Fiction
* **Main Idea:** A servant girl preserves her virtue and finally marries her master.
* **Main Characters:** Pamela Andrews, Mr. B.

---

## **Ivanhoe** – Walter Scott

* **Age/Era:** Romantic Age (19th Century)
* **Type:** Historical Novel
* **Main Idea:** Conflict between Saxons and Normans in medieval England; chivalry, honor, and nationalism.
* **Main Characters:** Ivanhoe, Rowena, Rebecca, Cedric, Richard the Lionheart.

---

## **Sons and Lovers** – D. H. Lawrence

* **Age/Era:** Modern Age (20th Century)
* **Type:** Psychological Novel
* **Main Idea:** Conflict between love, family, and individual desire; Oedipus complex.
* **Main Characters:** Paul Morel, Gertrude Morel, Miriam, Clara.

---

## **Lord of the Flies** – William Golding

* **Age/Era:** Modern Age (20th Century)
* **Type:** Allegorical Novel, Psychological Fiction
* **Main Idea:** A group of boys stranded on an island descend into savagery, showing the evil in human nature.
* **Main Characters:** Ralph, Jack, Piggy, Simon.
