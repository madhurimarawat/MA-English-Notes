# Writers Era, Age, Titles, Admirers, Critics, Contribution

# 🟣 Jonathan Swift

### 📅 Era & Age

* Augustan Age
* 17th–18th Century

### 👤 Life & Death

* Born: **1667**, Dublin, Ireland
* Anglican clergyman
* Dean of St. Patrick’s Cathedral, Dublin
* Died: **1745**

### 📚 Famous Works

* *Gulliver’s Travels*
* *A Modest Proposal*
* *The Battle of the Books*
* *The Drapier’s Letters*

### 🏆 Titles & Who Gave Them

* **“Prince of Satirists”** – literary critics

### 👏 Admirers

* Admired for powerful satire
* Influenced later satirists

### ⚖️ Critics

* Considered too bitter and harsh
* Accused of misanthropy

### 🌟 Contribution

* Perfected political satire
* Criticized corruption and human pride
* Strengthened prose satire tradition

# 🟣 Samuel Richardson

### 📅 Era & Age

* Augustan Age
* 18th Century

### 👤 Life & Death

* Born: **1689**, England
* Printer and publisher
* Died: **1761**

### 📚 Famous Works

* *Pamela*
* *Clarissa*
* *Sir Charles Grandison*

### 🏆 Titles

* **“Father of the English Domestic Novel”** – critics

### 👏 Admirers

* Praised for psychological depth
* Influenced later novelists

### ⚖️ Critics

* Criticized for excessive sentimentality
* Lengthy narratives

### 🌟 Contribution

* Developed epistolary (letter) novel
* Introduced psychological realism
* Focused on middle-class morality

---

# 🟣 Sir Walter Scott

### 📅 Era & Age

* Romantic Age
* Late 18th – Early 19th Century

### 👤 Life & Death

* Born: **1771**, Scotland
* Lawyer, poet, novelist
* Died: **1832**

### 📚 Famous Works

* *Ivanhoe*
* *Waverley*
* *Rob Roy*
* *The Lady of the Lake*

### 🏆 Titles

* **“Father of the Historical Novel”** – critics

### 👏 Admirers

* Influenced European novelists
* Popular worldwide

### ⚖️ Critics

* Style considered lengthy and descriptive

### 🌟 Contribution

* Popularized historical fiction
* Combined romance with history
* Revived interest in Scottish culture

---

# 🟣 David Herbert Lawrence (D. H. Lawrence)

### 📅 Era & Age

* Modern Age
* Early 20th Century

### 👤 Life & Death

* Born: **1885**, England
* Novelist, poet, essayist
* Died: **1930** (tuberculosis)

### 📚 Famous Works

* *Sons and Lovers*
* *Women in Love*
* *Lady Chatterley’s Lover*
* *The Rainbow*

### 🏆 Titles

* **Psychological Novelist of Passion** – critics

### 👏 Admirers

* Admired for psychological insight
* Influenced modern fiction

### ⚖️ Critics

* Accused of obscenity
* Works banned for sexual content

### 🌟 Contribution

* Explored human sexuality openly
* Focused on emotional and instinctive life
* Broke Victorian moral restrictions

---

# 🟣 William Golding

### 📅 Era & Age

* Modern / Postmodern Age
* 20th Century

### 👤 Life & Death

* Born: **1911**, England
* Schoolteacher and novelist
* Died: **1993**

### 📚 Famous Works

* *Lord of the Flies*
* *The Inheritors*
* *Pincher Martin*
* *The Spire*

### 🏆 Titles & Awards

* **Nobel Prize in Literature (1983)**
* Booker Prize (1980)

### 👏 Admirers

* Praised for symbolic fiction
* Studied widely in schools

### ⚖️ Critics

* Considered pessimistic
* Dark view of human nature

### 🌟 Contribution

* Explored evil in human nature
* Used symbolism and allegory
* Redefined post-war English fiction
