# Ivanhoe Characters

## 📘 **Ivanhoe Characters & Symbolic Representation**

### 🛡️ **Wilfred of Ivanhoe**

* **Role:** Protagonist, a Saxon knight.
* **Qualities:** Courageous, loyal, chivalrous, honorable; fights for justice and for love despite being disinherited by his father.
* Represents chivalric ideals and reconciliation between Saxons and Normans. 

### 👑 **King Richard I (The Lionheart)**

* **Role:** King of England, returning from the Crusades.
* **Qualities:** Brave, noble, adventurous; known for valor, but sometimes criticized for prioritizing personal glory over affairs of state.
* Often travels disguised as the **Black Knight**. 

### ❤️ **Lady Rowena**

* **Role:** Ivanhoe’s love interest and Cedric’s ward.
* **Qualities:** Beautiful, virtuous, loyal, mild‑mannered with inner strength.
* Embodies medieval ideals of womanhood. 

### 🕊️ **Rebecca**

* **Role:** Jewish healer, daughter of Isaac of York.
* **Qualities:** Intelligent, independent, compassionate, self‑controlled.
* Faces prejudice but remains strong; her love for Ivanhoe is deep though unconsummated. 

### 🛕 **Brian de Bois‑Guilbert**

* **Role:** Knight Templar antagonist.
* **Qualities:** Skilled fighter, morally complex; proud but ultimately torn by forbidden love for Rebecca.
* Serves as a dark contrast to Ivanhoe’s honor. 

### 🌲 **Locksley (Robin Hood)**

* **Role:** Leader of outlaws (the Merry Men).
* **Qualities:** Charismatic, heroic, skilled archer; champion of the oppressed and ally to Ivanhoe. 

### 🤴 **Prince John**

* **Role:** Richard’s brother and usurper.
* **Qualities:** Selfish, power‑hungry ruler who deepens political conflict in England. 

### 🐷 **Gurth**

* **Role:** Cedric’s swineherd and later Ivanhoe’s squire.
* **Qualities:** Loyal, hard‑working, desires freedom. 

### 🤡 **Wamba**

* **Role:** Court jester of Cedric.
* **Qualities:** Witty, humorous, loyal; often offers sharp insights beneath comic surface. 

### 🏰 **Cedric the Saxon**

* **Role:** Ivanhoe’s father.
* **Qualities:** Proud, traditional Saxon noble; initially prejudiced but eventually reconciles with Richard and Ivanhoe. 

### 💍 **Athelstane of Coningsburgh**

* **Role:** Saxon nobleman, Cedric’s chosen suitor for Rowena.
* **Qualities:** Peaceable but unremarkable; his claim stirs Saxon hopes for a restored dynasty. 

---

## 📗 **Themes Reflected Through the Characters**

* **Chivalry and Honor:** Embodied especially in Ivanhoe and Richard.
* **Prejudice and Tolerance:** Explored through Rebecca and Isaac’s struggles.
* **Unity and Division:** Saxon vs Norman conflicts drive the narrative.
