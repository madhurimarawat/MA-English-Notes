# 📖 Paradise Lost Book I

Author: John Milton
Published: **1667**
Book I Lines: **1–798**
Theme: **Fall of Satan and beginning of rebellion against God**

---

# 🌌 OVERALL SUMMARY OF BOOK I (VERY IMPORTANT)

Book I describes:

* Satan and fallen angels in Hell 😈
* Their defeat by God ⚡
* Satan’s pride and determination 🔥
* Plan to corrupt mankind 👤

**Central Idea:**
Even after defeat, Satan refuses to surrender.

---

# 🧾 IMPORTANT STANZA 1: Opening Invocation (Lines 1–26)

> “Of Man’s first disobedience, and the fruit
> Of that forbidden tree…”

---

## ✅ Meaning (VERY IMPORTANT)

Milton introduces the main subject:

* Adam and Eve’s disobedience 🍎
* Eating forbidden fruit
* Loss of Paradise

This caused:

* Sin 💀
* Death ⚰️
* Human suffering 😔

---

## 🔍 Symbolism

| Symbol             | Meaning              |
| ------------------ | -------------------- |
| Forbidden fruit 🍎 | Sin and disobedience |
| Fall               | Loss of innocence    |
| Paradise           | Perfection           |

---

## 🎯 Keywords

Fall of man • Original sin • Disobedience • Biblical theme

---

# 🧾 IMPORTANT STANZA 2: Invocation to Heavenly Muse (Lines 6–26)

> “Sing Heavenly Muse…”

---

## ✅ Meaning

Milton asks divine inspiration to help him write truth.

He wants to:

> “justify the ways of God to men”

---

## 🎯 MOST IMPORTANT EXAM LINE

**Milton’s purpose is to justify God’s actions and explain why evil exists.**

---

## 🔍 Symbolism

| Symbol        | Meaning            |
| ------------- | ------------------ |
| Heavenly Muse | Divine inspiration |
| Light         | Truth              |
| Darkness      | Ignorance          |

---

## Keywords

Divine inspiration • Religious epic • Moral purpose

---

# 🧾 IMPORTANT STANZA 3: Satan in Hell (Lines 50–83)

> “Him the Almighty Power
> Hurled headlong flaming from the ethereal sky”

---

## Meaning

God defeated Satan and threw him into Hell.

Satan fell due to:

* Pride 😈
* Rebellion ⚔️

---

## Symbolism

| Symbol           | Meaning              |
| ---------------- | -------------------- |
| Fall from Heaven | Punishment for pride |
| Hell fire 🔥     | Suffering            |

---

## Keywords

Pride • Punishment • Divine justice

---

# 🧾 MOST IMPORTANT STANZA: Satan’s Famous Speech (Lines 242–270)

> “Better to reign in Hell, than serve in Heaven”

---

## Meaning (VERY IMPORTANT)

Satan says:

He prefers ruling Hell over serving God.

This shows:

* Pride 😈
* Ego 🧠
* Rebellion ⚔️

---

## Symbolism

| Symbol | Meaning                 |
| ------ | ----------------------- |
| Hell   | Freedom with suffering  |
| Heaven | Authority and obedience |

---

## Exam Keywords

Pride • Ambition • Rebellion • Satan’s ego

---

# 🧾 IMPORTANT STANZA: Satan’s Determination (Lines 157–191)

> “What though the field be lost?
> All is not lost; the unconquerable will”

---

## Meaning

Even after defeat, Satan refuses to give up.

He still has:

* Willpower 💪
* Courage 🔥

## Keywords

Determination • Defiance • Psychological strength

# 🧾 IMPORTANT STANZA: Building Pandemonium (Lines 710–798)

Pandemonium = capital of Hell

Meaning of word:

Pan = all
Demon = demons

Pandemonium = place of all demons

---

## Symbolism

| Symbol      | Meaning         |
| ----------- | --------------- |

| Palace      | False power     |

## Keywords
