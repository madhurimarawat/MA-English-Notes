# Poets Admirers, Critics and Chronology

# 🟤 GEOFFREY CHAUCER : ADMIRERS & CRITICAL PRAISE

### 🔹 Important Admirers / Critics

* **Matthew Arnold**
  👉 Called Chaucer **“the Poet’s Poet”** ⭐ (VERY important)

* **John Dryden**
  👉 Called him **“The Father of English Poetry”**

* **William Hazlitt**
  👉 Praised Chaucer’s **human sympathy and realism**

* **Legouis**
  👉 Said Chaucer had **“free, fresh, and genial humour”**

### 🧠 Ready-made exam line:

> *Arnold famously called Chaucer “the Poet’s Poet” for his artistic perfection and human sympathy.*

---

# 🟣 JOHN MILTON : ADMIRERS & CRITICAL PRAISE

### 🔹 Important Admirers / Critics

* **John Dryden**
  👉 Said:
  **“Milton cuts us all out and the ancients too.”** ⭐⭐

* **Samuel Johnson**
  👉 Admired Milton’s **moral grandeur** (but criticised his style)

* **William Blake**
  👉 Said Milton was
  **“of the Devil’s party without knowing it”** (about Satan) ⭐

* **T.S. Eliot**
  👉 Criticised Milton’s influence on English poetry (negative but famous)

### 🧠 Ready-made exam line:

> *Dryden ranked Milton above both modern and classical poets for epic greatness.*

---

# 🟡 ALEXANDER POPE : ADMIRERS & CRITICAL PRAISE

### 🔹 Important Admirers / Critics

* **Samuel Johnson**
  👉 Called Pope
  **“The great model of correctness in English poetry.”** ⭐⭐

* **Matthew Arnold**
  👉 Admired Pope’s **clarity and precision**

* **Leslie Stephen**
  👉 Praised Pope’s **satirical genius**

### 🧠 Ready-made exam line:

> *Johnson regarded Pope as the supreme artist of the heroic couplet.*

---

# 🟢 WILLIAM WORDSWORTH : ADMIRERS & CRITICAL PRAISE

### 🔹 Important Admirers / Critics

* **S.T. Coleridge**
  👉 Praised Wordsworth’s **philosophy of nature**

* **Matthew Arnold**
  👉 Said Wordsworth’s poetry offers
  **“healing power”** to mankind ⭐⭐

* **Walter Pater**
  👉 Appreciated Wordsworth’s **spiritual depth**

### 🧠 Ready-made exam line:

> *Arnold believed Wordsworth’s poetry had a permanent moral and spiritual value.*

---

# 🟣 ALFRED LORD TENNYSON : ADMIRERS & CRITICAL PRAISE

### 🔹 Important Admirers / Critics

* **Queen Victoria**
  👉 Personally admired Tennyson (why he became Poet Laureate)

* **Matthew Arnold**
  👉 Called him
  **“The most representative poet of the Victorian Age.”** ⭐⭐

* **Stopford Brooke**
  👉 Praised his **emotional intensity and musical language**

### 🧠 Ready-made exam line:

> *Arnold considered Tennyson the true voice of Victorian doubts and hopes.*

# 📌 SEPARATE MASTER LIST : IMPORTANT CRITICS

* **Samuel Johnson** → correctness, moral judgement
* **John Dryden** → comparative criticism
* **William Hazlitt** → imagination & human nature
* **T.S. Eliot** → modern criticism (often negative)
* **Walter Pater** → aesthetic & spiritual reading

### 📜 English Poets in Chronological Order (by Age)

1. **Geoffrey Chaucer**
   *(Middle English Period : 14th century)*

2. **John Milton**
   *(Puritan Age / Late Renaissance : 17th century)*

3. **Alexander Pope**
   *(Neo-Classical Age / Age of Reason : early 18th century)*

4. **William Wordsworth**
   *(Romantic Age : late 18th to early 19th century)*

5. **Alfred Lord Tennyson**
   *(Victorian Age : 19th century)*
